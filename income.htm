Income Analysis

Income analysis is the review of the output of Airbnb listings. Airbnb market value of New York City is monitored through the total incomes of listings. Monthly income of each listing is estimated, following the notion of Airbnb. In this section, the users, particularly the Airbnb hosts in New York City, are able to see the income analysis in many dimensions in order to help them to make a change of management strategy for improving room services, prices, and others, based on the questions as follow:

  • What are the differences between Airbnb income of NYC and other cities in the US?
  • How does income by location (borough and neighborhood)?
  • How does income by room types and property types?
  • How does income by review ratings and availability?
  • How can the Airbnb hosts earn high income in NYC?

According to the notion of Airbnb, monthly income of each listing in New York City is estimated by estimated occupancy rate and daily price. Please click

Monthly income ($) = [Occupancy rate x (30 days x Daily price)]/100.
Yearly income ($) = Monthly income x 12 months

Income by Borough

The 51,097 Airbnb listings are in all 5 boroughs of New York City, including Manhatton, Brooklyn, Queens, Bronx, and Staten lsland. According to the interactive map in home page, Manhattan has the most number of listings, followed by Brooklyn. For the income issue, there are also differences between each borough as follows:

The bar chart with dropdown shows that Manhattan has the highest average monthly income at $3,066, followed by Staten Island with $1,687, while Bronx has the lowest average income at $1,375. In addition, yearly income is also plotted and has the same trend as monthly income. The listings in Manhattan have average income at $36,792

Income by Neighbourhood

Income by Room Type

The 51,097 Airbnb listings of NYC offer four different room types, including Entire house/apartment, hotel room, private room, and shared room. According to the interactive map in home page, entire house/apartment has the most number of listings, followed by private room, while the minority of them is hotel room. For the income issue, there are also differences between each room type as follows:

The bar chart with dropdown shows that hotel room has the highest average monthly income at $4,541, followed by entire house/apartment with $2,859, while shared room has the lowest average income at $1,283. In addition, yearly income is also plotted in each room type and has the same trend as monthly income. The listings in Manhattan have the highest average monthly income at $54,492

Income by Review scores rating and Availbility

The customers give review score rating for each Airbnb listings of NYC. This project divides review score rating into 3 levels, including high (>= 90.0), moderate (>= 60), and low (< 60). In addition, this project divides availability (all year round, excepting for when it is already booked) into 2 levels, including high (>= 60 days per year) and low (< 60 days per year). We found there are some difference about average listings price in each issue as follows:

The scatter chart with dropdown shows that the listings where get low review score rating have higher average monthly and yearly income than moderate and high scores listings. Moving onto availability, the high availability listings have average monthly and yearly income, higher than low availability listings.

The relationship between the monthly income and other variables

This tool is developed to find out the relationship between the income and other variables, including availability, daily price, estimated occupancy rate, and review scores.

According to the plot, there is positive relationship between monthly income and occupancy rate, as well as daily price. However, there are no relationship between income and reviewing scores rating, as well as availability.

Conclusion

  • The hosts in New York City has higher the average Airbnb monthly income than many big cities in the United States, such as Los Angeles, Washington D.C., San Francisco, Chicago, and Boston.

  • The listings in Manhattan can earn the highest average income compared to the other boroughs, following by Staten Island, while Bronx and Queen are the cheapest one. In addition, the listings where located near the bay can make more money than others, such as Manhattan Beach in Brooklyn and Fort Wadsworth in Staten Island, like in price analysis.

  • Hotel room can earn the highest monthly income compared to the other types, following by entire house/apartment, while shared room was the lowest one. To more consider about property, private room in boutique hotel can earn the highest income, while shared rooms in the bungalow and aparthotel are the lowest one.

  • Low review rating and high availability listings can earn the most income to the others.

  • The Airbnb hosts can earn high income if they have the listings where are in Manhattan with hotel room and entire house/apartment. They do not need to concern much about reviewing score and availability due to there are no relationship between income and reviewing score or availability.
