Price Analysis

Price analysis is the review of the interaction of service prices of Airbnb listings. All Airbnb hosts of New York City offer daily prices for the customers. However, there are some Airbnb hosts that also offer weekly and monthly prices. In this section, the users, particularly the visitors of New York City, are able to see the price analysis in many dimensions in order to help them to make a decision for finding the best temporary accommodations in New York City, based on the questions as follow:

  • What are the differences between Airbnb prices of NYC and other cities in the US?
  • How does price by location (borough and neighborhood)?
  • How does price by room types and property types?
  • How does price by review ratings and availability?
  • How can visitors avoid expensive housing prices in NYC?

Airbnb price in the United States

Inside Airbnb records Airbnb listings information of 26 big cities with 18 states in the United States. It seems that there are different service prices in each area, depending on the number of visitors, the number of housing, and economic size. According to the city map, the average Airbnb price in New York City is approximately $160.0 per night, lower than the average Airbnb price in the United States. We can see that New York City has lower the average Airbnb price than many big cites in the United States, such as Los Angeles, Washington D.C., San Francisco, Chicago, and Boston

Price by Borough

The 51,097 Airbnb listings are in all 5 boroughs of New York City, including Manhatton, Brooklyn, Queens, Bronx, and Staten lsland. According to the interactive map in home page, Manhattan has the most number of listings, followed by Brooklyn. For the price issue, there are also differences between each borough as follows:

The bar chart with dropdown shows that Manhattan has the highest average daily price at $215, followed by Brooklyn with $125, while Bronx has the lowest average price at $89. In addition, there are some Airbnb hosts who offer weekly and monthly accommodations for the visitors. To begin with weekly housing, Manhattan have the highest average weelky price at $1,113, followed by Staten Island with $782, while Bronx has the lowest average price at $455. Moving onto monthly housing, the rank of monthly price is the same as daily price, led by Manhattan with $3,682 and Brooklyn with $2,499, respectively.

According to the interactive map in home page, entire house/apartment has the most number of listings, followed by private room, while the minority of them is hotel room. For the price issue, there are also differences between each room type as follows:

Price by Review scores rating and Availbility

The customers give review score rating for each Airbnb listings of NYC. This project divides review score rating into 3 levels, including high (>= 90.0), moderate (>= 60), and low (< 60). In addition, this project divides availability (all year round, excepting for when it is already booked) into 2 levels, including high (>= 60 days per year) and low (< 60 days per year). We found there are some difference about average price in each issue as follows:

The scatter chart with dropdown shows that the listings where get high review score rating have higher average monthly and weekliy prices than moderate and low scores listings. However, the low score listings have the highest average daily price at $339 per night. Moving onto availability, the high availability listings have average monthly, weekly, and daily prices, higher than low availability listings.

The relationship between the daily price and other variables

This tool is developed to find out the relationship between the price and other variables, including availability, estimated number of bookings, estimated occupancy rate, and review scores.

According to the plot, there is positive relationship between price and reviewing scores rating, particularly in the score range of 60-100. However, there is negative relationship between price and number of bookings. Whereas, there are no relationship between the price and occupancy rate, as well as availability.

Conclusion

  • New York City has lower the average Airbnb price than many big cities in the United States, such as Los Angeles, Washington D.C., San Francisco, Chicago, and Boston.

  • Manhattan has the most expensive rentals compared to the other boroughs, following by Brooklyn, while Bronx was the cheapest one. In addition, the listings where located near the bay and had the good view are the most expensive rentals, such as Manhattan Beach in Brooklyn and Fort Wadsworth in Staten Island.

  • Hotel room has the most expensive rentals compared to the other types, following by entire house/apartment, while shared room was the cheapest one. To more consider about property, private room in boutique hotel has the highest service price, followed by entire housing boat.

  • High review rating and high availability listings has the most expensive compared to the others.

  • NYC visitors who would like to avoid high housing costs payment. They should choose the listings where are far from Manhattan and Brooklyn with shared room, low reviewing score, and low availability.
