Activity Analysis

Activity analysis is the review of the activity about Airbnb customers and hosts. This part focuses on the number of reviews, reviewing score rating, availbility, the number of hosts, and the host response rate. In addition, the number of bookings and occupancy rate are estimated and analyized to see Airbnb activity of the customers. In this section, both the customers and hosts are able to see the activity indicators analysis in order to help them to make a decision for finding the best temporary accommodations in New York City and improve quality of housing for boosting reviewing scores and occupancy rates, based on the questions as follow:

  • What are the differences between Airbnb activities of NYC and other cities in the US?
  • How does occupancy rate and review ratings by location and room type?
  • How does number of Airbnb customers vary by location and room type?
  • How does availability of listings vary by location, room type, and review ratings?
  • Are there Airbnb super-hosts in NYC, and what makes a host as Super host?

The Occupancy Model

The occupancy model can be used to estimate how often an Airbnb listing is being rented out, and also approximate a listing's income. According to the notion of Airbnb, the reviews per month, the number of minimum nights, and the number of average stay night in NYC are used to estimated the number of bookings, the number of nights, and occupancy rate.
